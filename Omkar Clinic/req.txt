About Us
Located in the heart of Hadapsar, Pune, Omkar Clinic & Sonography Centre is a trusted healthcare destination led by Dr. Anil Lonkar (M.D. Obst/Gynae) and Dr. Aparna Lonkar (M.B.B.S., DA).
